How Ellevux works

No setup headaches, no tech skills needed. We build your AI receptionist, it answers every call, and you get more booked jobs. Here's the whole thing in plain English.

In four steps

1 We set it up for you

Tell us about your business: your services, your hours, and the questions you get asked most. We build and train your AI receptionist and put it on your number. You don't touch any settings.

2 It answers every call, 24/7

Day or night, weekends and holidays, it picks up in under 12 seconds and sounds like a real member of your team. Most callers never realize they're talking to AI.

3 It qualifies the lead and books the job

It asks the right questions, works out what the caller needs, and books the appointment straight into your calendar. No double-bookings, no leads slipping through the cracks.

4 You see everything

Every call, summary, and booked job shows up in your dashboard. If someone calls after hours and doesn't book, your AI texts them a booking link on its own. See a live demo of the dashboard →

What your AI handles

Everything a great front-desk person would do, working around the clock.

Sounds like your business

It's trained on your business name, your tone, and your FAQs, so every call feels on-brand. Callers won't know it's AI unless you tell them.

Sends calls to the right place

One phone number, multiple locations. It routes each caller to the right team, location, or calendar based on where they're calling from.

Follows up by text

If a caller doesn't book on the call, your AI follows up by text over the next day or two, with a few gentle reminders, until they book.

Books into your calendar

It syncs with your Google Calendar, respects your real availability, and books appointments directly, so you're never double-booked.

Logs every lead

Every call and booking is written into your CRM automatically, with the caller's details and what they needed. No manual data entry on your end.
