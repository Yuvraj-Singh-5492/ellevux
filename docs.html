Getting Started

Authentication

Learn how to securely log in, or — for developers — how to generate API keys so you can connect your own custom software to Ellevux without ever exposing your main credentials.

Call Routing

Tell the AI exactly what to do with different types of calls — by writing plain-English rules.

"If the caller says emergency, forward to my cell. If they want a quote, send them a pricing text."

Integrations

Connect Ellevux to the software tools you already use to run your business.

Google Calendar

Connect your business calendar so the AI knows exactly when agents are available and can book appointments directly — entirely avoiding double-booking.

HubSpot

HubSpot is one of the most popular CRMs for local businesses. This integration lets Ellevux automatically log call transcripts, check client histories, and insert new leads directly into your pipeline — no manual data entry.

Advanced

Webhooks

A webhook is a way for Ellevux to instantly send data to another app. For example, you could ping a Slack channel with a call summary the moment a customer hangs up the phone.
